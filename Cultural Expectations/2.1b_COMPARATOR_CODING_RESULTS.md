## VERDICT ON G-1 (per pre-registered decision rule ¬ß3)

**Observation across the comparators:** CFO 3/3 composite. COO 4/4 composite (control fires). CMO
composite where it exists (MorseLife scale + documented growth), structurally absent early. No
comparator at any stage scored **bounded** (tension-resolving scope limits). Every captured C-suite JD
demands ‚â•2 (in fact 3) of the role's own modes, unbounded.

**This matches decision-rule row 1:**
> **CFO/CMO/COO are mostly composite (‚â•2 modes, unbounded), like the CTO ‚Üí CTO-specificity is FALSE.**

### ‚Üí G-1 VERDICT: **CTO-specificity is FALSE (as worded).** The composite is a **C-suite-wide** framing.

The composite ‚Äî deep functional craft + people leadership + external/board strategy, held
simultaneously and unbounded ‚Äî is **not** specific to the CTO. It appears in the CFO (the supposedly
*most-bounded* role) at every stage, in the COO (control) at every stage and flavor, and in the CMO
wherever the role exists. **T2 must be restated** from a CTO-specific claim to: *"C-suite hiring
artifacts apply a context-free composite standard."* Draft A's existing hedge to "a claim about C-suite
hiring artifacts generally" is **confirmed correct**, no longer a placeholder.

### Secondary (stage interaction, per ¬ß3): SUPPORTED
Composite-ness is retained across stages for every role coded (CFO early‚Üíscale; COO early‚Üíscale). This
is *additional* evidence the framing is **structural and C-suite-wide**, not CTO-specific.

### Two refinements the coding surfaced (do NOT overstate the verdict beyond these)

1. **The composite differs in KIND, even if present in all (links Run A, verified).** "Present in all"
   ‚âÝ "identical in all." Verified research shows the CFO de-composes by delegating **bounded modes**
   (Controller owns accounting, FP&A owns forecasting ‚Äî cleanly, permanently), whereas the CTO/eng
   delegates **labor** (VP Eng) while technical modes stay **fused**. So the JD-level composite is
   C-suite-wide (G-1 false), but the *lived separability* of that composite may still differ by role.
   This is the surviving CTO-distinctiveness candidate (**mode-separability**), and it is a DIFFERENT
   claim from G-1 ‚Äî it must not be smuggled in as if G-1 supported it.
2. **The identity-rupture rationale for CTO-specialness is REFUTED (Run C, verified).** The IC‚Üímanager‚Üí
   exec identity rupture is a **universal** craftsman‚Üíexecutive pattern (sales QJE 2019, academia,
   surgery, Hill), not tech-specific. Any CTO-specific claim must NOT rest on "the identity transition
   is uniquely hard." The only live distinctiveness routes are **mode-separability** (verified) and
   **churning-craft / can't-bank-it** (untested).
